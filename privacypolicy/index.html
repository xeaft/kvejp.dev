Privacy Policy | 30.03.2024

We do not store any of your user data. We can't do so, as we do not own a place such as a server database to store it. All settings are saved in the browser local storage, which is saved on your machine and your machine only. Any data in local storage are not shared with anyone, and are not being used to identify a person. We do not even have any way of seeing it for ourselves.

You can find all stored data in the "Application" page of the developer tools, specifically in the "Local storage" section under the "Storage" category. The location may vary depending on the browser you're using.

There is no such thing as a registration or any other way of sending any of your data with us.

We use Cloudflare for website security and performance optimization purposes. This includes the protection of our website against security threats such as DDoS attacks, as well as the caching of website content to improve loading times and overall user experience. Cloudflare may collect and process certain data in accordance with their privacy policy.

We want to reassure you that through Cloudflare, we only have access to the network connection of our website. This information does not include any personal data about you, except for the country from which the connection is made. Please note that this country information is not linked to your identity and will never be used to identify you as an individual.

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. Any updates to the privacy policy will be effective immediately upon posting the revised version on our website. We encourage you to review this privacy policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your information.

Since we do not have any way of directly contacting users, we are unable to notify you individually about changes to the privacy policy. However, by continuing to use our service after any changes are made, you acknowledge and agree to the updated privacy policy.

We will indicate the date of the last update at the top of this privacy policy for your reference. Your continued use of our service after any modifications to the privacy policy constitutes your acceptance of those changes.

For any further details contact us at support@kvejp.dev
